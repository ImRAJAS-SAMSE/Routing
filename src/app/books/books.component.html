Recommended Books for Various Technologies

Full-stack Web Development

  • "Eloquent JavaScript" by Marijn Haverbeke
  • "You Don't Know JS" by Kyle Simpson
  • "Clean Code: A Handbook of Agile Software Craftsmanship" by Robert C. Martin

App Development

  • "The Pragmatic Programmer: Your Journey to Mastery" by Dave Thomas and Andy Hunt
  • "Mobile App Development with React Native" by John Niedzwiecki
  • "Android Programming: The Big Nerd Ranch Guide" by Bill Phillips and Chris Stewart

Programming Languages (Java/C++/Python/Ruby/Rust)

  • "Effective Java" by Joshua Bloch (Java)
  • "The C++ Programming Language" by Bjarne Stroustrup (C++)
  • "Python Crash Course" by Eric Matthes (Python)
  • "Eloquent Ruby" by Russ Olsen (Ruby)
  • "Programming Rust" by Jim Blandy and Jason Orendorff (Rust)

Video Editing

  • "In the Blink of an Eye" by Walter Murch
  • "The Filmmaker's Handbook" by Steven Ascher and Edward Pincus
  • "Adobe Premiere Pro Classroom in a Book" by Maxim Jago
